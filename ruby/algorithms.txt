
Use an elevator
 
You are on floor 0
Go to the elevator
Face the elevator 
Find the buttons
    If you are going up 
    press the up button
    If you are going down
    Press the down button 
wait until the elevator arrives and opens
Enter the elevator
Find the button with the floor number you are going to
Press it by     
    count 1 up from the first button until the number equals to the number of the floor you want
    Press that button

Wait for elevator to stop at floor chosen
An led screen will display the floor the elevator is at
    If elevator stops
    And it is not the chosen floor , wait
    or if the elevator stops 
    And it is the chosen floor , get off -mission accomplished!
    Or Else if the elevator stops and does not open
press the help button
